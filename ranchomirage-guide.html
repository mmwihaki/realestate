Rancho Mirage Neighborhood Guide

Some of our favorite spots for food, drinks, groceries and activities in Rancho Mirage

Casual bistro with picturesque views offers varied Californian cuisine, plus an all-day happy hour.

Babe’s Bar-b-que & Brewhouse | 171800 CA-111 Ste A 176, Rancho Mirage, CA 92270

Beers brewed on the premises help wash down baby-back ribs & pulled pork, plus scratch-made pies.

Serving classic American dishes served alongside martinis and a swingin’ lounge act.

Dringk Eatery + Bar | 71800 CA-111, Rancho Mirage, CA 92270

Restaurant and bar with a fun atmosphere.

Serving authentic Italian cuisine specializing in fresh made in-house pasta, house-made sausage, meatballs, and sauce.

Fitness & Wellness

Offers group interval fitness classes, a one-of-a-kind workout broken into intervals of cardiovascular and strength training.

Free Things To Do

  • Visit the Rancho Mirage Library & Observatory
  • Take a short walk through Cancer Survivors Park
  • Take a tour of Sunnylands Center & Gardens, a calming 200-acre estate with carefully landscaped desert gardens.
  • Catch a show under the stars at the amphitheater of Rancho Mirage Community Park
  • Go hiking in Indian Canyons, a group of canyons featuring trails for hiking & horseback riding, plus picnic areas & a gift shop.
  • Take a drive out to Joshua Tree National Park
  • The Living Desert Zoo and Gardens is not free, but features over 1,000 acres of desert ecosystem and plenty of animals.
  • About a 30-minute drive out is the Palm Springs Aerial Tramway which gives you a 360-degree view of the surrounding valley, plus opportunities for hiking.

Hiking
