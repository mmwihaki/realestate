Desert Neighborhood Guide

Some of our favorite spots for food, drinks, groceries and activities in The Desert

Restaurants

Rooster and the Pig | 356 S Indian Canyon Dr, Palm Springs, CA

Incredible industrial-chic Vietnamese American fusion.

Workshop Kitchen + Bar | 800 N Palm Canyon Dr, Palm Springs, CA

Stylish New American eats in a former movie theatre.

Purple Palm | 572 N Indian Canyon Dr, Palm Springs, CA

Located inside the Colony Palms Hotel.

King’s Highway Diner | 701 E Palm Canyon Dr, Palm Springs, CA

Eclectic, locally sourced Cali-Mex fare located inside the Ace Hotel.

Shabu Shabu Zen and Sake Bar | 71680 Hwy 111, Rancho Mirage, CA

Contemporary Japanese hotpot and tapas.

Tac/quila | 415 N Palm Canyon Dr, Palm Springs, CA

Tacos and tequila!
