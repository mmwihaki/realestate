Redondo Beach Neighborhood Guide

Some of our favorite spots for food, drinks, groceries and activities in Redondo Beach

Restaurants

Captain Kidd’s Fish Market & Restaurant | 209 N Harbor Dr, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Fresh & cooked-to-order lobster, salmon & other seafood sold in counter-serve environs with a patio.

Redondo Beach Crab House | 100 Fishermans Wharf F, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Spacious mainstay with outdoor seating & ocean views offers broad seafood & alcoholic drink menus.

Sea Level Restaurant & Lounge | 655 N Harbor Dr, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Airy restaurant & lounge inside the Shade Hotel for a seafood-centric menu, wine & water views.

Eat At Joe’s | 400 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Bustling, long-running diner serving big portions of American comfort food in a convivial setting.

The Standing Room | 144 N Catalina Ave, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Easygoing burger joint serving up hand-packed patties in no-frills digs with outdoor patio seating.

Dominique’s Kitchen | 522 CA-1, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Well-appointed eatery offering a range of French-Italian dishes paired with select wines.

Good Stuff Restaurant | 1617 S Pacific Coast Hwy #102, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Breakfast-dinner Californian-American cafe offering wraps, salads & under-600-calorie menu.

Original Pancake House | 1756 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Chain serving signature pancakes & other American breakfast classics all day in dinerlike digs.

Wildflower Cafe | 600 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Artful, counter-serve American cafe for breakfast & lunch with coffee, beer & indoor/outdoor seats.

Bluesalt Fish Grill | 2515 Artesia Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Cozy & casual eatery serving up a variety of grilled seafood choices, as well as tacos & salads.

Take Out

Quality Seafood | 130 International Boardwalk, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Open since 1953, this stalwart shop offers fresh fish & seafood plus meals to eat in the picnic area.

Jus’ Poke | 501 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Small restaurant serving a limited menu of Hawaiian specialties in a no-frills setting.

Redondough | 1218 Beryl St, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Coffee, baked goods and sandwiches just right for breakfast and lunch.

Blazin’ Hens | 2315 Artesia Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Serving up premium quality Nashville hot chicken.

Fatta a Mano | 610 Torrance Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Italian take out created for people who appreciate traditional homemade pasta made from quality ingredients.

Phanny’s | 1021 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Popular for breakfast burritos & California-inspired dishes in a chill space with counter service.

Sacks on the Beach | 1611 S Catalina Ave #120, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Colorful American cafe offering sandwiches, salads, juices & coffee, plus a patio with water views.

Zayna Flaming Grill | 1310 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Cafe with Middle Eastern flair dishes up familiar favorites, including hummus, falafel & shawarma.

Taishi Hainan Chicken | 1000 Torrance Blvd ste b, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

A spot for Asian fusion street food specializing in Hainan Chicken Rice.

Coffee

Kirari West Bake Shop | 707 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Warm, casual outpost serving gluten-free sandwiches, baked goods, pastries & coffee drinks.

The Boy & The Bear | 350 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Specialty coffee roastery on a direct relationship with Colombian coffee growers.

Klatch Coffee | 306 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Cafe that has won awards for their coffees, espresso, signature drinks, and customer service.

Krust Bakery & Cafe | 1300 S Pacific Coast Hwy Ste108, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Fresh-baked breads & pastries, breakfast & lunch items and coffee crafted by a local roaster.

Hi-Fi Espresso | 1815 S Catalina Ave, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Cafe specializing in espresso drinks, also offering vegetarian dishes.

La Terraza | 1201 S Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Bringing LA coffee culture to the South Bay, this cafe serves coffee, tea, breakfast and brunch.

Kona Koffee Company | International Boardwalk, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Serving Kona coffee from Hawaii, as well as coffee drinks, smoothies and teas.

Groceries

Smart & Final Extra! | 615 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Warehouse-like supermarket chain for produce, meats & packaged foods, plus discounts on bulk items.

Whole Foods Market | 405 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Eco-minded chain with natural & organic grocery items, housewares & other products.

Vons | 1212 Beryl St, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

SoCal supermarket staple for groceries, produce & deli items, plus personal care, home goods & more.

Trader Joe’s | 1100 Pacific Coast Hwy, Hermosa Beach, CA 90254

Grocery chain with a variety of signature items, plus produce, dairy & more.

Grocery Outlet | 2115 Artesia Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Discount chain carrying brand-name groceries, plus items like gifts & toys.

Sprouts Farmers Market | 1515 Hawthorne Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Specialty chain offering a range of natural & organic grocery items.

Shopping

Redondo Beach Pier | 121 W Torrance Blvd #103, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Landmark pier known for scenic oceanfront dining, shopping & nightlife, plus music & festivals.

Riviera Village | 1728 S Catalina Ave, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Hub for fashion, art, boutique, and restaurants. A central place to shop, dine and relax.

Hermosa Avenue | 1221 Hermosa Ave, Hermosa Beach, CA 90254

Boutique shopping, dining and happy hours along Manhattan Beach.

Fitness & Wellness

Allegiate Gym | 1421 Aviation Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Founded by two former USC football players and their coach, this gym focuses on expert coaching, a high energy atmosphere, and quality strength training.

Triton Gym | 1400 Aviation Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

Specializes in HIIT training, boxing, kickboxing, self defense, and MMA classes.

Body One Fitness | 201 Herondo St #100, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Group training facility with offerings such as Hi-Volume, Cross Training to Plyometric Training classes.

King Harbor Fitness | 1008 N Catalina Ave, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Gym with martial arts and cardio classes focused on providing a solid, well-rounded workout.

Orangetheory Fitness | 409 N Pacific Coast Hwy #205, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Energizing group interval fitness classes that are broken into intervals of cardiovascular and strength training using a variety of equipment.

Melt RX Fitness | 531 N Pacific Coast Hwy, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Offers 1-hour group HIIT classes that will keep your calories burning for hours afterwards.

iLoveKickboxing | 1262 Beryl St, Redondo Beach, CA 90277

Kickboxing studio offering group classes.

Anytime Fitness | 300 Pacific Coast Hwy, Hermosa Beach, CA 90254

Gym with 24-hour access and offering personal training and classes like bootcamp, kickboxing, and spin.

Free Things To Do

  • Enjoy the view from Redondo Beach Pier.
  • Catch some sun and waves on Redondo Beach.
  • Visit a memorial to local veterans in Veterans Park, a green space overlooking the ocean.
  • On Thursdays from 8am-1pm, shop the Redondo Beach Farmer’s Market for produce, baked goods, plants & more.
  • During the summer, visit Seaside Lagoon to swim and play - great for kids.
  • Have a picnic and enjoy the nature at Hopkins Wilderness Park.
  • Go hiking with ocean views at Palos Verdes Estates Shoreline Preserve.
  • Stroll along picturesque Miramar Park with an elevated view of the ocean.
