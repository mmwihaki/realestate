Property Description

The flagship development project by Open Air Real Estate. With the architecture completed by David Hertz and inspired by a one hundred year old avocado tree, this home has been freshly renovated from the ground up. With 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms just blocks from the ocean, this home is the model for California living.


Venice Beach is a vibrant, friendly community with a history for attracting artists and creative-minds. The house is just blocks from everything you could want for entertainment and shopping, including fine-dining restaurants, fashion boutiques, prestigious wine shops, unique art galleries and a great nightlife.


One of the biggest perks of this home is that it is a 30-second walk away from the legendary Canyon Country Store. You can grab almost anything during your stay last minute, from an amazing latte to sandwiches or alcohol. The Canyon Country Store is a local favorite, and having it a 30-second walk away allows you the opportunity to enjoy this beautiful house without ever having to jump in your car and deal with the Los Angeles traffic!
