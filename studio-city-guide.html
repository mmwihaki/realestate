Studio City Neighborhood Guide

Some of our favorite spots for food, drinks, groceries and activities in Studio City

Restaurants

Ca Del Sole | 4100 Cahuenga Blvd, Toluca Lake, CA 91602

Rustic, stylish restaurant offering traditional Italian antipasti, fresh pastas & a vast wine list.

SUGARFISH | 11288 Ventura Blvd C, Studio City, CA 91604

Celebrated local sushi chain serving traditional rolls & sake in a chic, intimate space. One of the many great sushi restaurants in Studio City.

Firefly | 11720 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Upmarket American cuisine with a library-like foyer, a hopping bar & a romantic patio dining area.

GRANVILLE | 12345 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Local chain serves up a big menu of classic American cuisine in a sun-soaked, laid-back setting.

Mercado | 3413 Cahuenga Blvd W, Los Angeles, CA 90068

Sleek, contemporary digs doling out creative Mexican meals, plus more than 70+ tequila options.

The Six Chow House | 12650 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Bustling gastropub in a rustic space offering 6 menu items to choose from in each course.

Laurel Tavern | 11938 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Cool gastropub showcasing burgers & other fare plus craft beers in woody digs with outdoor seating.

The Front Yard | 4222 Vineland Ave, North Hollywood, CA 91602

All-day spot for modern Californian fare and cocktails, located inside The Garland Hotel.

LALA’S Argentine Grill | 11935 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Cozy, casual eatery with outdoor seating offers a mix of Argentinean & American cuisines.

JINYA Ramen Bar | 11239 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Casual branch of a local chain specializing in Japanese noodle soup, located in a shopping center.

Take Out

Casa Vega | 13301 Ventura Blvd, Sherman Oaks, CA 91423

1950s throwback offering late hours, Mexican eats & margarita-fueled happy hours.

In-N-Out Burger | 3640 Cahuenga Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90068

Classic California-based burger chain serving customizable patties, hand-cut fries & thick shakes.

Joe’s Falafel | 3535 Cahuenga Blvd W, Los Angeles, CA 90068

Quick-service Mediterranean-inspired eatery featuring falafel, kebabs & many vegetarian dishes.

Tere’s Mexican Grill | 11548 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Serving up traditional Michoacán favorites like homemade corn tortillas and flavorful carne asada.

Carney’s Restaurant | 12601 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Longtime fast-food shack serving burgers, hot dogs & fries since 1975 in a vintage railcar.

All About Poke | 13203 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Serving generous portions of poke atop rice or salad with multiple side orders and toppings.

Spice Season | 11402 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Rustic-chic eatery using sustainable ingredients to make Southeast Asian dishes with a twist.

H.O.P.E. | 11943 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Bright, modern cafe serving vegan & vegetarian Thai cuisine with meat substitutes & brown rice.

Salt & Straw | 12180 1/2 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Popular ice cream chain out of Oregon known for inventive flavors & farm-sourced ingredients.

Coffee

Aroma Coffee & Tea Co. | 4360 Tujunga Ave, Studio City, CA 91604

Cozy cafe with breakfast, sandwich, salad, pastry & coffee options plus backyard patio seating.

M Street Coffee | 13251 Moorpark St, Sherman Oaks, CA 91423

Coffee purveyor serving organic blends, smoothies & baked goods & displaying local artists.

Alfred Coffee | 12077 Ventura Pl, Studio City, CA 91604

Local coffee chain serving innovative coffee and tea drinks as well as sweet & savory bites.

Coffee Fix | 12508 Moorpark St, Studio City, CA 91604

Easygoing coffee shop offering espresso drinks, breakfast & lunch sandwiches, plus free Wi-Fi.

Red Window Coffee | 12953 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Pint-sized coffee stand with window service doling out classic beverages & baked goods.

Peet’s Coffee | 12215 Ventura Blvd #101, Studio City, CA 91604

California-born chain featuring signature coffee, tea & blended drinks alongside baked goods.

Groceries

Ralphs | 10901 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Standard grocery store with a variety of brand name items.

Vons | 4033 Laurel Canyon Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

SoCal supermarket staple for groceries, produce & deli items, plus personal care, home goods & more.

Trader Joe’s | 11976 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Grocery chain with a variety of signature items, plus produce, dairy & more.

Shopping

Ventura Boulevard | 12144 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

A bustling strip of vintage stores, strip malls, restaurants and bars that runs through the middle of Studio City.

Universal CityWalk | 100 Universal City Plaza, Universal City, CA 91608

High-energy hub of shops, eateries & nightclubs plus entertainment such as a cinema & bowling alley.

Sherman Oaks Antique Mall | 14034 Ventura Blvd, Sherman Oaks, CA 91423

Enduring indoor antique mall featuring 100 dealers selling vintage decor, furniture, jewelry & more.

Fitness & Wellness

Training Mate Studio City | 12429 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Modern fitness facility with weights, cardio machines & high-intensity interval training classes.

Horsepower Fitness | 11559 Ventura Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Offering fun fitness classes & training programs with diverse, scalable workouts.

CrossFit Studio City | 4010 Colfax Ave, Studio City, CA 91604

Say goodbye to the machines, treadmills and monotonous routines. Certified coaches deliver carefully programmed workouts that will take you to an entirely new level of fitness.

Built Gymnasium | 4070 Laurel Canyon Blvd, Studio City, CA 91604

Members have unlimited access to all fitness classes including themed spinning, TRX, and pilates.

GoTribe Fitness | 4444 Lankershim Blvd, North Hollywood, CA 91602

Specializes in fitness classes, nutritional coaching, stress management and their own unique fitness data tracking app and software.

LIFT Society | 3270 Cahuenga Blvd W, Los Angeles, CA 90068

A boutique lifting gym where the focus is aesthetics. Class sizes range from 6-8 people per class.

Reel Fitness | 12215 Ventura Blvd #107, Studio City, CA 91604

A full-service gym featuring a staff of experienced professional trainers.

Free Things To Do

  • Every Sunday from 8am to 1pm, The Studio City Farmers’ Market is held on Ventura Place, between Laurel Canyon Blvd and Radford Ave.
  • Drive by The Brady Bunch House
  • Take a stroll through Coldwater Canyon Park
  • Walk through Studio City by the North Valleyheart Riverwalk

Hiking

  • Fryman Canyon Trail in Fryman Canyon Park
  • Dearing Trail in Wilacre Park
