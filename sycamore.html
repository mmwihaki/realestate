Property Description

This is a lovely duplex located in Los Angeles between San Vicente and 12th Street. The downstairs unit, 1207, is the original house. It includes 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a formal dining area, and an updated kitchen with a retro stove and modern appliances, granite countertops and tiled flooring. The living room is perfect for entertaining and includes a cozy reading nook which has a fireplace as the focal point. The dining area can comfortably seat a group of eight.The rear unit, 1209, was constructed in 2016. It was built with energy efficiency in mind. This home has one bedroom and one and a half baths.

 

There is laminated wood flooring throughout, with tile in the kitchen and bath. On the first floor of this unit is the living room, kitchen, and one half bath. Up the staircase to the second floor features an office area where you can work from home and house your book collection. Also on the second level, is the bedroom, a full bath which is tiled from floor to ceiling and rock flooring in the shower.There is a special treat up the next staircase. It features a meditation area. Exit the door to discover the roof top where you can enjoy 360 degree views of the city
