Beverly Hills Neighborhood Guide

Some of our favorite spots for food, drinks, groceries and activities in Beverly Hills

Restaurants

Il Cielo | 9018 Burton Way, Beverly Hills, CA 90211

Fresh Italian fare in an enchanting garden setting. The perfect date spot.

The Rooftop by JG | 9850 Wilshire Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Restaurant with New American fare by Jean-Georges, located on the Waldorf Astoria rooftop.

Prospect Gourmand | 105 N Robertson Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90211

Upscale California-inspired menu with farm fresh ingredients.

Yazawa | 9669 S Santa Monica Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Yakiniku (Japanese barbecue) with the best wagyu beef from Japan.

Il Pastaio | 400 N Canon Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

An upscale and charming Italian restaurant with fresh, homemade pasta.

Fogo de Chão | 133 N La Cienega Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90211

Upscale Brazilian steakhouse with all-you-can-eat meat carved tableside.

Avra Beverly Hills | 233 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Greek restaurant with great seafood dishes.

Backyard Bowls | 8303 Beverly Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90048

Delicious açaí bowls, super-friendly staff and fresh ingredients. Possibly the best in Beverly Hills!

Mastro’s Steakhouse | 246 N Canon Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Highly acclaimed steakhouse - also try The Penthouse at Mastro’s on the 3rd floor for live music and a slightly more laid-back setting.

Take Out

Urth Caffé Beverly Hills | 267 S Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90212

European-style cafe with healthy dishes and drinks (coffees, juices, and teas).

Nate’n Al’s | 414 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

The quintessential LA deli, open since 1945! Best for breakfast and lunch.

Tokyo Seven | 456 N Bedford Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Well-packed sushi boxes. They also have a ramen burger and bento boxes.

Upper Crust Pizzeria | 243 S Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90212

Award-winning pizzeria using fresh, local ingredients.

Il Tramezzino | 454 N Canon Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Italian cafe with the best paninis. Also serves salads, pastas, and desserts imported from Italy.

Otro Dia Tacos | 9909 CA-2, Beverly Hills, CA 90212

Best authentic tacos in Beverly Hills.

Soom Soom Fresh Mediterranean | 9533 Santa Monica Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

A fast-casual restaurant offering gluten-free Mediterranean street food.

Coffee Shops

Blue Bottle Coffee | 132 S Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90212

Well-known and trendy cafe chain.

Brighton Coffee Shop | 9600 Brighton Way, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Also serves classic breakfast and lunch fare as well as Korean specialties. Open since 1930.

Nespresso Boutique | 408 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Sip some tasty coffee while shopping for your Nespresso accessories.

Alfred Coffee | 490 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Whimsical, smaller coffee shop also selling food items. Great place to catch up with friends.

Verve Coffee Roasters | 8925 Melrose Ave, West Hollywood, CA 90069

The coffee shop for coffee enthusiasts. Get there early and grab a seat to study or work in.

Cafe Sheera | 443 N Bedford Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Luxury cafe selling a variety of desserts to go with your coffee.

Grocery & Bodegas

Beverly Hills Market & Deli | 303 N Crescent Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Quaint local market with a full-service deli.

Trader Joe's | 8500 Burton Way, Los Angeles, CA 90048

Grocery chain with a variety of signature items, great prices for food and wine.

Whole Foods | 239 N Crescent Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Big eco-minded chain with natural and organic grocery items.

Artesia Fresh Produce Market | 2322 Artesia Blvd, Redondo Beach, CA 90278

This recently renovated neighborhood market is a local gem. The produce is super fresh and very reasonably priced, the marinated meats are fantastic, and the sandwiches and coffee are delicious!

Shopping

Rodeo Drive | 295 Rodeo Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90212

Famous high-end shopping street with every designer brand you can think of.

TWEAK / Best Gift Store Ever | 8384 Beverly Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90048

Interesting collection of gifts and stationery items.

Taschen | 354 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Variety of art books and gallery by the publisher of the same name.

Beverly Center | 8500 Beverly Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90048

High-end shopping mall with designer shops and food court.

The Cheese Store | 419 N Beverly Dr, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Fancy artisanal cheeses, charcuterie, and wine. Pick up a gift basket or do a tasting.

Fitness & Wellness

Namastday Yoga | 113 N San Vicente Blvd, Fl 2, Beverly Hills, CA 90211

Experienced teachers from all over the world, wonderful staff, and classes for all skill-levels.

Easton Gym | 8053 Beverly Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90048

No frills gym with great classes and easy parking.

Oak Fitness Club | 9346 Civic Center Dr #145, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

HIIT and Pilates classes in a private, group, or DIY setting.

Health House | 605C N W Knoll Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90069

Beautiful facility with classes that kick your butt. Bonus: they have a juice and smoothie bar.

Bolder Fitness | 8810 W Pico Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90035

Gym with 24-hour access for keycard holding members. Personal trainers and on-site physical therapist available.

Mansion Fitness | 8358 Sunset Blvd, West Hollywood, CA 90069

Luxury gym decked out with chandeliers and baroque mirrors.

Gainz Athletic Facility | 8733 W 3rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90048

Not your typical gym workout. Personalized training and small, intimate classes available.

Rise Nation | 613 N La Cienega Blvd, West Hollywood, CA 90069

Home to the first 30-minute full body workout class on a Versaclimber that made it to one of Men’s Health’s top 10 workouts in the fitness industry.

LIFT Society | 7300 Sunset Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90046

Boutique lifting gym with focus on aesthetics. Semi-private small classes available.

MERGE | 930 S Robertson Blvd unit d, Los Angeles, CA 90035

Zero impact HIIT workouts for total body fitness in a studio with LED lighting, disco ball, and great music.

Free Things To Do

  • Window shop on Rodeo Drive.
  • Visit Greystone Mansion, a historic mansion featured in hundreds of movies and TV shows. The grounds and gardens are open to the public from 10am-5pm daily.
  • Stroll Beverly Gardens Park and see their various gardens or take a photo at the iconic Beverly Hills sign.
  • Walk down Melrose Avenue, one of LA’s most famous streets for dining, entertainment, and shopping. If you feel like viewing some art, MOCA Pacific Design Center is a free gallery along this street.
  • Walk by the Spadena House, also known as The Witch’s House - because that’s exactly what it looks like! Located on the corner of Walden Drive and Carmelita Avenue.
  • Hiking - Franklin Canyon
