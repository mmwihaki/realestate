Meet The OA Real Estate Group Agents

Austin Victoria

REAL ESTATE AGENT

Austin began his career in real estate after working under one of the top producing agents in Los Angeles. This experience allowed him to gain the knowledge necessary to serve his clients at the highest level. Being born and raised in California with a deep love for Los Angeles, he found a career in real estate to be the perfect fit. Austin seamlessly transitioned to real estate after previously working as a national and international male model and actor based in Los Angeles. The skills he learned in that industry helped him succeed at working with new clients, quickly adjusting based on their wants and needs to get a successful end goal.

Austin prides himself on putting his clients first and doing whatever it takes to have a smooth and rewarding transaction. No pressure, no sales, just honest and clear communication. His goal is to handle the entire transaction and to let his clients sit back and relax. He is a part of the Beverly Hills Greater Los Angeles Association of Realtors, the California Association of Realtors and the National Association of Realtors.

Email: austin@oarealestategroup.com

Philip Glenn

REAL ESTATE AGENT

Philip began his real estate career in New York, NY working with some of the top producing agents in sales and rentals, helping to provide clients with the individual attention they require and deserve. Prior to joining the real estate world Philip worked for a Fortune 500 financial firm on Wall Street, giving him the business background needed for a successful real estate career.

With his passion for architecture and design, which began at a young age, Philip fell in love with the diverse architecture of Southern California, from the mid-century to super modern to even the classic Spanish revival. After many visits and vacationing in Palm Springs, he knew this was the right place to continue his career in real estate. Undeniably, he feels Southern California is amazing for its medley of people and its diverse landscape that cannot be found anywhere else.

Philip brings with him an approachable and effective communication style. His passion and tenacity push him to see a job through from start to finish. Philip’s goal is to make sure that his clients feel confident and secure every step of the way all while bringing them service beyond the sale or purchase. His reputation and that of the individuals that he assists is of paramount importance, earning him a high level of trust for his commitment to integrity and discretion. Philip is part of the Palm Springs Regional Association of Realtors, the California Association of Realtors and the National Association of Realtors.

Phone: 917-208-7129

Email: philip@oarealestategroup.com
